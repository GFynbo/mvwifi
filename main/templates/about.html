Chappaquiddick Wireless Internet Service Provider

Chappy WISP (mvWiFi, llc) is a “last mile provider” for Internet and related services and started commercial operation June 1, 2009 after a year of testing. We operate under the Telecommunication Act of 1996, with the goal of providing high-speed Internet service to the entire island of Chappaquiddick and surrounding harbors. We own and operate the only tower on the island of Chappaquiddick. From our 85’ tower we receive bandwidth from the Vineyard as well as two feeds from a fiber line in Falmouth. After going through our secure servers we transmit to our customer’s locations via six sector arrays and four dedicated relay feeds.
